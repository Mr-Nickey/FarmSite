Our Story: Built with Recycled Hands

We believe in making beautiful things from what others leave behind. Our chicken coops, and even our farm stand, are proudly built using all reclaimed and recycled materials. We repurpose and reuse everything we can!

This philosophy extends to how we care for our animals. Our chickens and two pet geese live a happy life, free-ranging and enjoying the fresh New Hampshire air. They're the heart of our homestead! (Please note: we don't sell geese eggs at this time.)
