Meet the Flock

Our farm wouldn't be the same without our feathered family. The chickens roam freely, foraging and enjoying the fresh air, which is our secret to amazing eggs.

And you can't miss our two resident geese! They're our beloved pets and the official farm greeters, keeping a watchful eye on their chicken companions. They're all part of what makes our farm so special.
